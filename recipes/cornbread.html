Sweet Golden Cornbread

Description

This cornbread recipe is made from scratch with no mix. It's a real treat with chili, ribs, or barbecued chicken.

You could even use this sweet, homemade cornbread to make stuffing for your Thanksgiving feast! My mom made this for me as a child, and now it's my family's favorite.

Serve with butter, jam, honey, or your favorite spread.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Add the wet ingredients.
  3. Whisk until well-combined.
  4. Pour in a prepared cake pan.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
