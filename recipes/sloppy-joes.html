Sloppy Joes

Description

These crowd-pleasing Sloppy Joe sandwiches will take you back to your childhood!

This is my mom's recipe and it always gets compliments!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onions
  • Tomato based souce
  • Hamburger buns

Steps:

  1. Cook the beef, onion, and green pepper.
  2. Drain the liquids, then stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Simmer for about 30 minutes.
  4. Serve on hamburger buns.
